# Service Tracks

The four-track model is the core of how Alphabyte structures AI engagements. Each track answers a specific question, has its own entry conditions, and can be entered independently — clients don't need to start at Track 1.

This file extracts the public-safe content. For full internal detail (effort estimates in hours, full deliverable lists, internal sales scenarios), consult `reference-doc.docx`.

## The four tracks

### Track 1 · Discovery

**Question it answers:** "What should our AI strategy be?"

**What it is:** Stakeholder workshops, use case identification, feasibility analysis, AI roadmap. A focused, time-boxed engagement that produces a clear path forward — not a pile of recommendations.

**Who it's for:**
- Companies with no prior AI investment
- Leadership that wants strategy clarity before committing budget
- Teams curious about AI but unsure where ROI lives

**Timeline:** 3–5 weeks
**Investment:** $14K–$22.5K (fixed price)
**Public name for the productized version:** Strategy Sprint

**Key deliverables (public-safe framing):**
- Stakeholder workshops with leadership and operating teams
- Use case prioritization
- Feasibility and gap analysis
- Findings & Recommendations roadmap

### Track 2 · Data Readiness

**Question it answers:** "Is our data ready for AI?"

**What it is:** Data quality audit, governance assessment, AI readiness scorecard, remediation pathway. AI projects fail more often because of data than because of models — this track makes that risk visible before you spend on infrastructure.

**Who it's for:**
- Companies where data quality is unknown or suspect
- Regulated industries with compliance requirements
- Teams about to begin agent or integration work

**Timeline:** 4–8 weeks
**Investment:** $15K–$40K

**Key deliverables (public-safe framing):**
- Data quality audit
- Governance assessment
- AI readiness scorecard across multiple dimensions
- Remediation pathway

### Track 3 · Enablement

**Question it answers:** "How do our people use AI?"

**What it is:** Two productized offerings — one for leadership, one for teams. The fastest path to visible ROI in most engagements.

**Two offerings within the track:**

**Executive Productivity Suite** — Custom Claude environment for leadership. Knowledgebases, skills libraries, prompt toolkits. Deployed in weeks, not quarters.
- Timeline: 2–4 weeks
- Investment: starting at $7K

**Team Citizen Dev Enablement** — SDLC plugin, guardrails, enablement workshop, graduation playbook. For teams already using Claude informally and ready to do it properly.
- Three tiers: Starter (3–5 weeks), Standard (6–10 weeks), Enterprise (8–12 weeks)
- Investment: starting at $15K (Starter) up through enterprise rollouts

**Who it's for:**
- Leadership wanting a fast, visible AI win
- Teams already using Claude in scattered or ungoverned ways
- Existing Alphabyte data/BI clients ready for an AI lift

**Public-friendly framing of the offerings:**
- Executive Productivity Suite is the highest-converting entry point — but in public copy, describe it as the fastest way for leadership to start using AI in their actual work, not as a sales conversion device.
- Team Citizen Dev Enablement turns informal AI use into governed capability — in public copy, frame as "informal Claude use is already happening; this turns it into something durable."

### Track 4 · Infrastructure

**Question it answers:** "How do our systems use AI?"

**What it is:** Where AI stops being a productivity tool and becomes operational infrastructure. Custom MCP servers, autonomous agents, on-premise LLM deployments, fine-tuned custom models.

**Four offerings within the track:**

**Agentic Systems & Custom MCP** — Custom MCP servers connecting Claude to internal databases, APIs, CRMs, ERPs. OAuth 2.0, governed connections. You own the server.
- Timeline: 4–8 weeks
- Investment: $18K–$36K

**Custom AI Agents & Systems** — Autonomous agents that handle defined workflows end-to-end. Command Centre dashboard, human-in-the-loop approval gates where the stakes require it.
- Timeline: 8–16 weeks
- Investment: $35K–$90K

**On-Premise LLM Deployment** — Llama, Mistral, or other open-source models on client infrastructure. For clients where cloud AI is ruled out by data sovereignty or security policy.
- Timeline: 10–18 weeks
- Investment: $45K–$90K

**Fine-Tuned Custom LLMs** — Domain-specific model trained on proprietary data. Production deployment, MLOps, governance.
- Timeline: 20–36 weeks
- Investment: starting at $90K

**Who it's for:**
- Companies ready to connect AI to live internal systems
- Operations that need autonomous workflow handling
- Clients where data sovereignty rules out cloud AI

## Tracks aren't sequential

Important framing point: **clients don't need to do tracks in order.** Discovery isn't a prerequisite for Enablement. The tracks are designed to be entered where the client's actual problem lives.

In public copy, lean into this — it's a real differentiator from competitors who insist on full top-to-bottom programs. The line "you don't need all four tracks; you need the right one, for where you actually are" is genuinely on-brand and worth using (or rephrasing in voice).

## Entry bundles

Productized starting points — fixed-price, time-boxed, fast to begin. These are the marketable on-ramps.

### Strategy Sprint
- **Track:** Discovery
- **What it is:** Fixed-price, time-boxed discovery. Stakeholder workshops, use case prioritization, AI roadmap. Answers "where do we actually start?" without a full programme commitment.
- **Investment:** $14K–$22.5K (fixed)
- **Timeline:** 3–5 weeks
- **Leads to:** Any track

### Executive Quick Win
- **Track:** Enablement (Executive Productivity Suite)
- **What it is:** Custom Claude environment for the leadership team in weeks. Knowledgebases, skills library, prompt toolkit. Visible ROI before anything else is scoped.
- **Investment:** $7K–$18K
- **Timeline:** 2–4 weeks
- **Leads to:** Infrastructure or Citizen Dev

### Sprawl Fix
- **Track:** Data Readiness + Enablement + Infrastructure (MCP)
- **What it is:** For teams already using Claude in silos. Governed data marts, custom SDLC plugin org-wide, MCP connection to data, graduation playbook. Turns sprawl into managed capability.
- **Investment:** $60K–$100K
- **Timeline:** 10–14 weeks
- **Leads to:** Infrastructure (Agents)

## Intelligent Enterprise — full-programme offer

For mature organizations committing to full-scale autonomous AI deployment across operational workflows. All four tracks, phased delivery with go/no-go checkpoints between phases.

- **Tracks covered:** Discovery, Data Readiness, Enablement (Executive Suite + Citizen Dev), Infrastructure (MCP + Agents)
- **Investment:** $89K–$190K+
- **Timeline:** 24–40 weeks · phased delivery
- **What clients get:** Full AI roadmap, data readiness scorecard, executive AI environment, citizen dev rollout, custom MCP infrastructure, production agents, Agent Command Centre dashboard
